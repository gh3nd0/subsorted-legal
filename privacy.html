Privacy Policy for SubSorted

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SubSorted (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles your information. SubSorted is an iOS application that helps you track your subscriptions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

The short version

SubSorted stores all of your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. We do not have servers that receive your information, we do not use analytics or tracking, and we have no access to anything you enter into the app.

What data the app stores

When you use SubSorted, you may enter information such as subscription names, prices, billing dates, and related notes. This information is stored only on your device using Apple’s on-device storage. It never leaves your device and is never sent to us or to any third party.

Because this data lives only on your device:

  • We cannot see it.
  • We cannot access it.
  • We cannot recover it for you if it is lost (for example, if you delete the app or your device).

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. SubSorted does not include analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or crash-reporting tools that send information off your device.

How your data is removed

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling SubSorted removes all data the app has stored on your device.

Apple App Store

While we do not collect data, Apple may collect certain information when you download or use apps from the App Store (for example, purchase and usage statistics) in accordance with Apple’s own privacy policy. This is outside our control. You can read Apple’s Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Your rights under UK GDPR

Because all of your data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, you remain fully in control of it at all times. You can view, edit, or delete your data directly within the app at any time.

Under UK GDPR you have rights including the right of access, rectification, erasure, and to object to processing. As we do not hold or process any of your personal data, these rights are effectively exercised by you directly through the app. If you have any questions about your data, you can contact us using the details below.

Children’s privacy

SubSorted is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
